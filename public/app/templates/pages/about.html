Welcome to Fix Me!

FixMe uses the infermedica artificial intelligence to quickly you give a diagnosis based on your symptoms.

Enter your chief complaints in the diagnosis section, and infermedica will respond with a series of follow-up questions. Once infermedica believes you have a 50% or greater probability of having a condition, she will diagnose you. You can continue answering questions to get a more accurate diagnosis.

Coming soon! Once you have a diagnosis, you will then have the option to find an appropriate doctor near you to assist you further.

Please note this application is solely for educational purposes and NOT a replacement for your doctor. Please make an appointment with your physican for a proper consultation.
